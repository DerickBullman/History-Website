Statistics

Over 16,405,000 flights are handled by the faa every year. That's 45,000 per day. They handle over 10,000,000 scheduled passenger flights yearly, with 2,900,000 passengers flying in and out of us airports daily. There are 167,000 fixed wing aircraft affiliated with the faa with 25,506,000 flight hours per year. 44,500,000,000 pounds of freight are moved per year and flights generate $488 billion of annual revenue.
